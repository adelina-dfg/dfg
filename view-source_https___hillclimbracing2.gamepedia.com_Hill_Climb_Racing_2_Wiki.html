<div class="body">
<p><b><a href="/Hill_Climb_Racing_2" title="Hill Climb Racing 2">Hill Climb Racing 2</a></b> is the sequel to the most addictive and entertaining physics based driving game ever! And its free!
</p><p>Bill Newton is Back! After journeying where no driver has been before, Bill is ready to challenge the whole world in Hill Climb Racing multiplayer madness.
</p><p>Face new unique challenges in unique environments with many different cars. Defeat your opponents and collect big bonuses to tune your car and reach ever higher positions. With little respect to the laws of physics, Bill Newton will not rest until he has conquered the highest hills!!
</p>
